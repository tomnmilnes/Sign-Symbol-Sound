ROLODEX PROPAGANDA


Rolodex Propaganda is a exhibition exploring propaganda, corruption, deep fakes, and deception. The show is an online/offline 'embassy' of The Wrong Biennale existing as a mobile-only exhibition on Polygon Palm. The online PP show displays an interactive virtual rolodex of artist images, scrollable by viewers, that link to their artist/work pages. The exhibition will have a physical manifestation at KARST, Plymouth, UK in March 2024. The physical show includes a giant rolodex visible and scannable by viewers who can access the AR and online artifacts.
