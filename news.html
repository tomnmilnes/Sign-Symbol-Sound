OPEN CALL
For Digital/NetArt commissions

Polygon Palm is a digital art platform for desktop and mobile-only exhibitions. Check out the current exhibitions on www.polygonpalm.com. We want to see engaging and intriguing online works that communicate a critical understanding of the internet to explore the digital platform. Artists will work with digital processes, exploring boundaries of technology and art, and will be supported in creating their works for Polygon Palm. Polygon Palm currently has two opportunities for commissions on its platforms:

Polygon Palm - Mobile-only online exhibitions.
Polygon Plaza - Desktop/laptop-only exhibitions.
