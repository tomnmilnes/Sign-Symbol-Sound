Bittersweet Memory by Nichole Shinn is an explorative three part WebGL game that reflects on the malleable nature of memory. In a post cataclysmic event where no world exists anymore, a ghost traumatized by the sudden destruction reflects upon and struggles to rebuild what they remember about their life lived.

This project expresses the surreal and otherworldly nature of building monuments in your mind, especially in response to coping with death and disaster.

These WebGL games are load heavy and work best in browsers like Chrome and Firefox with higher speed internet.
For controls you can use arrow keys or W,A,S,D and spacebar to interact. Camera changes with movement.
